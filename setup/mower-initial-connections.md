- Raspbery Pi: Version 4 B.

- Temperature Sensor: [DFRobot Waterproof DS18B20 Temperature Sensor Kit](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B07434MB77/ref=ppx_yo_dt_b_search_asin_title?ie=UTF8&psc=1)
